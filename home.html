Welcome to Our Nest

Little Scholars Next Preschool is a home-based licensed preschool in San Ramon, CA that has been providing excellent child care and preschool education to the Tri-Valley area since May of 2007.

  • Nominated for 2012 Readers Choice Award ‘Best Preschool in San Ramon’ by the San Ramon Patch!
  • Our graduates are the first students to skip kindergarten and advance to first grade at all local elementary schools in San Ramon.
  • Our Little Scholars are introduced to topics outside the primary classroom including: gymnastics, dance, basic computer tools, and foreign languages!
  • We teach your child public speaking and stage exposure from a young age through our weekly "Show and Tell" program.

Our Vision and Mission

At Little Scholars Nest we believe that the early years of a child’s life are the most significant period, as this is when the foundation is laid for the overall development of the individual.

Structured Reading Program for Growing Scholars

Our carefully designed reading program introduces our Little Scholars to complex reading concepts at a steady pace making them advanced readers by the time they leave the nest for kindergarten.

Extracurricular Engagement for Boosting Imagination

Outside of preschool learning, your child will have exposure to dance, gymnastics, computer skills, foreign languages, and art and crafts which gives them the unique opportunity to begin exploring hobbies and interests at a young age.

Our Nest is a Home Away from Home

Our group of children and teachers bond closely every year growing together in our personality and academic development. Here at Little Scholars Nest, your child will develop a bond with their classmates and teachers that will last for a lifetime.
